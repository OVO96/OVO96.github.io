What I can do

Web Design

Based on briefs provided by the client, I create digital prototypes to match the client(s) vision. This is essential in ensuring the final product is in line with the clients wishes and desires.

Web Development

Once I've created the digital prototypes as per the client's specifications, I embark on the major task which is creating the website from the initial design. Using HTML, CSS and (a bit of) JavaScript, I ensure that the site is developed as requested. As part of my routine work, I ensure the website is optimized for speed, performance, accessibility and search engine optimization(SEO).

WordPress

Who wouldn't want to easily manage all the content and multimedia on their website? When connecting the website to Wordpress, I pay special attention to the usability and intuitiveness of the interface being created. I use plugins that make it as easy as possible for the end user to administer the site.

Official Emails

For small businesses, strong online presence is critical and nothing appears more professional than having official emails to accompany your business name.This informs potential customers that you are a serious business and they will be more willing to work with you.

Website Maintenance

After designing and developing a client's website, I ensure the site is up and running on the internet,and at a small fee, I continue performing maintenance and answering any questions the client might have pertaining to the workings of the website.
